Process

Your Data Analysts

4 Simple Steps

How We Work With You

Often we work with companies that simply don’t have the bandwidth or confidence to conduct regular analysis. Without the actionable insights, growth can stall or even decline as the competition increases. Our team not only manages the data, we're able to pull the actionable insights to inform strategy, tactics and actions to drive your business forward.

1. Strategy 2. Plan 3. Measure 4. Support

1. Strategy

We kick off with a deep dive, split into two phases. The first phase is to understand your business goals and the results of the activity to date. The second phase ensures all data is accurately captured and centralised into one location. This allows us to immediately interrogate your data, pinpointing what’s already working and what’s not.

2. Plan

The key to optimising performance is to ensure you have a measurement plan in place. Over a 2 - 3 hour session, we work with you to identify priorities, craft KPIs and confirm the key business questions, whose answers will inform, strategy, tactics and actions. This information defines what your dashboard will contain - but don’t worry, your dashboard will evolve as the business progresses.

3. Measure

Based on the measurement plan, we first design your dashboard prior to building it. Presenting what your dashboard will look like ensures, the team fully understands each component part, what they represent, and how the dashboard will be used. With feedback, we’re able to create the best possible presentation that the team already knows how to use. Once built, we’re on hand to support your team as they become familiar with it. Decisions become informed and KPIs are monitored, keeping teams, vendors and even freelancers on track.

4. Support

Every 3 months, over a 2-3 hour session we review benchmarks together, discuss new challenges, craft new KPIs, and update your dashboard as needed or even create an entirely new one. We know KPIs change and goals move and new problems arise as you grow. That’s why we constantly keep on top of your data, meeting regularly to ensure your questions are answered, KPIs are measured, dashboards are evolved and you are able to change your behaviour in order to outgrow the competition.

Measurement Metrics

Inform Strategy, Tactics And Action

Your data can provide powerful insights that will change the way you behave. Even if you don’t know exactly what to ask of your data yet, we can help you think about the key questions you should ask, and what actions you might take as a result of the data.

Actionable
Metrics

Change your behaviour by helping you pick a course of action.

Leading
Metrics

Give you a predictive understanding of the future.

Causal
Metrics

Cause one metric, like revenue, to change via something you can control, like an ad.

Exploratory
Metrics

Are speculative and find unknown insights that give you the upper hand over competitors.
