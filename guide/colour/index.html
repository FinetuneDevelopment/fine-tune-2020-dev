Colour

Light colours

Light colours use the bt- (background tint) prefix in their classnames.

bt-white
$colour-white
#fff

bt-smoke
$colour-smoke
#f4f4f4

bt-muted-blue
$colour-muted-blue
#daebf6

bt-pink
$colour-pink
#fee2dc

bt-mint
$colour-mint
#d7f2db

Dark colours

Dark colours use the bg- (background) prefix in their classnames.

bg-grey
$colour-grey
#666

bg-interactive-blue
$colour-interactive-blue
#2e38ef

bg-deep-blue
$colour-deep-blue
#02023D

bg-black
$colour-black
#010111
